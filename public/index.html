Rules
Every player will have a Dating Name and Bio which is publicly displayed. Every player will also have a Real Name which is a name hidden from the public. The more Real Names of the players you know, the more you'll be at an advantage.

The objective of this game is to earn as many Hearts (points) as you can.

Each round is 1 min 30 secs long. And in every round, each player has an option choose up to 3 people to date.

Players earn points from the following:
If you date someone and they also date you, NO Hearts are earned by either party. Instead, the other player's Real Name is revealed to each other.
If you date someone and they choose not to date you, they have rejected you. They earn 1 Heart while you earn NO Hearts. However, since you were rejected, that player's Real Name is revealed to you.
If you do not choose to date anyone, you lose 2 Hearts for being single.

Every player also has a Target Date whose Real Name is revealed (not their Dating Name). If you successfully dates your target, meaning your target also chooses to date you, you earn 5 Hearts and have a new target.
However, if you choose to date your Target but are rejected, you still earn 3 Hearts and have a new target.
